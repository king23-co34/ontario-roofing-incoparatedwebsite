About Ontario Incorporated

We're not just a roofing company. We're a team of dedicated professionals committed to protecting Toronto homes with quality, integrity, and excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2011, Ontario Incorporated began with a simple mission: to provide Toronto homeowners with professional, reliable roofing and exterior services they can trust.

What started as a small team has grown into a respected name throughout the GTA, known for our attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and customer-first approach.

Today, we're proud to have completed over 500 successful projects, each one reflecting our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

From small repairs to complete roof replacements, we approach every project with the same level of professionalism and care.
